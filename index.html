Daily Maestro — Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 3, 2026

In short: Daily Maestro does not collect, transmit, sell, or share any personal data. Everything you create in the app stays on your device. We have no servers, no accounts, no analytics, and no tracking.

1. Data we collect

We do not collect any data. Daily Maestro has no user accounts, no backend servers, no analytics, and no advertising or tracking. We never receive your information.

2. Data stored on your device

The app stores the following only on your device:

  • Practice recordings (audio you record)
  • Songs, sections, chords, keys, tempos, and practice notes you enter
  • Imported sheet music (PDFs or photos) and backing-track audio files you add
  • Practice session logs, routines, setlists, goals, and app settings

This data never leaves your device unless you explicitly choose to share a file (for example, via AirDrop, Messages, or the Files app). Any such sharing is initiated and controlled entirely by you.

3. Device permissions

The app may ask for the permissions below. Each is used only on your device for the stated purpose, and granting it does not send any data to us:

  • Microphone — to record your practice sessions.
  • Camera — to scan paper chord charts and sheet music.
  • Photo Library — to import sheet-music images.
  • Apple Music (Media & Apple Music) — to play along with songs and import your playlists, through Apple’s MusicKit, using your own Apple Music account.
  • Bluetooth — to connect wireless MIDI controllers and keyboards.

4. Third-party services

  • Apple Music / MusicKit: When you search, play, or import Apple Music content, that interaction is handled by Apple on your own Apple account and is governed by Apple’s Privacy Policy. We do not receive this data.
  • Music charts: The app fetches public, anonymous “popular songs” chart data from Apple (iTunes) and Deezer to show suggestions. These requests contain no personal information or identifiers.
  • Tutorial links: If you open a tutorial video link (for example, YouTube), it loads from that provider and is governed by that provider’s privacy policy.

5. Children’s privacy

The app does not collect personal information from anyone, including children.

6. Data retention and deletion

Because your data is stored only on your device, you remain in control of it. Deleting the app removes its data from your device. You can also delete individual recordings, songs, and sessions from within the app at any time.

7. Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, we will update this page and revise the effective date above.

8. Contact

Questions about this policy? Contact us at tee.pelissier@gmail.com.
