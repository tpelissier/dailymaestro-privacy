Getting started

  • Pick your instrument on the Home tab, then add a song from the “Learn Most Popular Songs” charts or create your own.
  • Break a song into sections (Verse, Chorus, Bridge…) and set its key and BPM in Song detail.
  • Practice — open a song and tap play to start the timer and metronome. Your key, section, and chords stay in view while you play.
  • Build a routine on the My Routine tab and step through it with Play Routine mode.

Common questions

How do I record my practice?

Tap the record button in a practice session. Recordings are saved on your device and play back from the Log tab. Grant the microphone permission when prompted.

How do I add sheet music or chords?

Use “Add Content” in a practice session or Song detail to import a PDF, a photo, or a ChordPro / plain-text chord chart. You can also type chords directly per section.

Can I play along with the original song?

Yes — link a song to its Apple Music track and play along in the practice screen. Full-song playback requires an active Apple Music subscription; without one, you’ll hear a 30-second preview. Apple Music playback uses Apple’s MusicKit on your own Apple account.

How do I connect a MIDI keyboard?

Connect via USB, or pair a wireless controller under Settings → “Bluetooth MIDI Devices.” MIDI notes play the loaded instrument and drive the chord and interval trainers.

Can I use my own instrument plugins (AUv3)?

Yes. Daily Maestro can host third-party AUv3 instruments installed on your device. Open the instrument tool in a practice session and pick one. If no third-party AUv3 instruments are installed, the picker will be empty — the built-in metronome and tools still work.

Where is my data stored?

Everything stays on your device — there are no accounts and no servers. See our Privacy Policy for details. Deleting the app removes its data; you can also delete individual recordings, songs, and sessions in-app.

What are the requirements?

Daily Maestro requires iOS / iPadOS 17.6 or later.
